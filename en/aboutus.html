Stephanie brings her passion to the table.
NoYo is headed by Stephanie Mages. A native of New York City, Stephanie brings passion to the table. Her commitment to innovate and to create new and unique experiences and celebrations suits events of any type. For many years, Stephanie worked for a premier catering company in New York City. Her experience ranges from planning corporate galas for thousands, to intimate parties for family and friends. She eventually found a specialty in wedding planning and currently lives in Abensberg with her young family. Stephanie now brings her New York sensibilities to Germany and to all of the special events she plans.
